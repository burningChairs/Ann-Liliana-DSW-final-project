Welcome to Number Guess!


Number Guess is a fun and engaging web game created by Ann Jordan and Liliana Aguilar.




How To Play



Guess the number in 6 tries!


Each guess must be valid numbers from 0-99.

The color of the text box for that guess specifically will change its colors to show how close your guess was to the real number.

If you refresh, your answers won't save!



Examples:



2

The guess is not close to the real number.


75

The guess is getting closer to the real number.


84

The guess is correct! This is the real number.
